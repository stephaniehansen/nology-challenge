About Me

A little about my story.

Originally from Sydney, Australia, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from the University of Technology, Sydney. Through my degree I learnt Java and C++ where I majored in Enterprise Systems Development. After completing my studies, I spent a few years living and working in the United States before moving to Bristol in 2019.

My passion for technology began at the age of 12, where I taught myself to code and built my first website. I continued building websites as a hobby throughout my school years, before landing a position as a web content manager. Through my career, I have gained experience in content management, user experience design and project management.
